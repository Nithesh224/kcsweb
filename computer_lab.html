Computer Lab description

The School has two computer laboratories with 60 computers accessible to students. It has advanced infrastructure in terms of hardware and software which cater to the requirements of the students, teachers and the curriculum. The school is equipped with a dedicated broadband Internet connection for enterprising students to get a chance to become familiar with the Internet. It also allows for comprehensive research for their projects and presentations.

The computer department has qualified and committed teachers to teach different classes at different levels i.e. Primary, Secondary and Senior Secondary. The faculty members make continuous efforts to ensure that the students gain expertise in the practical implementation of the tools they have in their curriculum. The Information Technology curriculum has been planned in such a way that the students of classes III to VIII would be exposed to various technologies and software. In the primary section, the students are trained to use basic software like Windows, MS-Office (Ms-Word, MS-Powerpoint, Ms-Excel etc.). Along with this, they are trained in computer programming at elementary stage through the computer languages like LOGO and Q-Basic in order to improve their logical skills. The students are assessed on their practical skills over a regular time interval via projects and assignments given to them. In the higher classes the students have an option to learn advanced software which are the part of their curriculum. The Students get exposure to the latest Multimedia software like FLASH, Photoshop, and Image-Ready etc. On the other hand, to sharpen their logical and reasoning skills, they learn computer languages like Visual Basic, JAVA and C++. Besides what is prescribed in the curriculum, the students are given opportunities to participate in various competitions allied with Information Technology.
