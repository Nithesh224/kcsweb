Social Science Lab


Social Science Lab description

The primary purpose of social studies is to help young people develop the ability to make informed and reasoned decisions for the public good as citizens of a culturally diverse, democratic society in an interdependent world. KCS has the Social Science Lab to inculcate the above values through the below points. To increase the interest of students in the subject. The teachers should keep in touch with the problems of the students related to Social Science and try to solve it with their best efforts. To improve civic sense among the students by creating awareness about the society. To help students understand changes that occurs with the changing time in the society and engage with the Institutions that shape their lives. Students should understand that “The progress of the human society is a continual and flexible progress”. To make students capable of making an effective and important contributions to the development of society. To produce knowledge seekers, fit and tough for the 21st Century. To provide sound knowledge of social organization, political and economic structure and civics ethics.
