Language Lab description

The language laboratory is an audio-visual installation used as an aid in modern language teaching. It helps the students to gain better exposure towards language. It provides the privacy that encourages the shy students to speak without hesitation. The significance of the language laboratory is felt in the domain of communication.

In our school, we introduced the language lab where the children played an active role in developing their pronunciation, accent, stress and all other aspects of phonetics. Students were facilitated with interactive resources that provoked their interest towards creative thinking and developing their linguistic abilities. The use of language lab debunked the myth of overcoming the obstacles in learning a language by providing supplementary materials. The structure of the lab facilitated the students with greater volume of interactive resources. It had been very interesting for the students to acquire the language abruptly and effortlessly.
